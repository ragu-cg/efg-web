PAYMENT & REFUND POLICY
================================================

PAYMENT POLICY
--------------
1. All payments can be made by crossed cheque payable to EFG Training Services
   Private Ltd, Bank Transfer, PayNow, or SkillsFuture Credit.
2. Payment must be made on or before the course commencement date.
3. If payment is not received, the printing of the ID Pass / Certificate will
   not be possible.
4. For retake exams, the full course fee applies.
5. Singaporeans & PRs: if a grant is not approved by SSG, full payment must be
   made by the participant.


REFUND POLICY
-------------
1. Payment is required upon receipt of confirmation.
2. If a written notice of withdrawal is received by EFG at least one week before
   the course starts, a 90% refund will be provided.
3. If the notice of withdrawal is received less than one week but more than three
   working days before the course starts, a 75% refund will be issued.
4. No refund will be given if EFG receives the notice of withdrawal within three
   working days before the course starts.
5. No refund will be issued if the participant fails to attend the course on the
   commencement date or discontinues mid-way through the course.
6. Participant replacement is allowed if the request is made prior to course
   commencement.


================================================
By booking with EFG Training Services, you acknowledge that you have read,
understood, and agreed to comply with the above payment and refund policy.
